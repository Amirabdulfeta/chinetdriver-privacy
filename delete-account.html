Delete Account – Chinet Driver

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Chinet Driver app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your choice. Your personal information, documents, and job history will be deleted immediately.

Some anonymous usage data and logs may be retained for 30 days for system and compliance purposes.

If you have any questions, contact us at your@email.com.
